Understanding Autism

Autism, or Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), is a neurological condition that affects how individuals perceive the world and interact with others. Each autistic person is unique, with their own strengths and challenges.

How to Communicate Effectively

  • Be Clear & Direct: Avoid vague language. Say exactly what you mean.
  • Be Patient: Some autistic individuals may take longer to process information.
  • Use Visual Aids: Images, gestures, or written instructions can help with understanding.
  • Respect Their Space: Some people with autism may prefer less physical contact.
  • Listen & Observe: Pay attention to body language and non-verbal cues.
  • Avoid Sensory Overload: Loud noises or bright lights can be overwhelming.
