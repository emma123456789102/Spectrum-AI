Frequently Asked Questions

What is autism?

Autism, or Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), is a developmental condition that affects communication, social interaction, and behavior.

How can I support someone with autism?

Be patient, use clear communication, and respect their unique needs and preferences.

Are there treatments for autism?

There is no cure for autism, but therapies and interventions can help individuals develop skills and manage challenges.
